Achievements

Mr.Aavinash Payal is one of the finest choreographer ,performer and Teacher in the field of Entertainment and Education. Aavinash is a theatre artist and has worked on many theaterical dance drama, theme based plays as choreographer,performer and also as an actor in English,Hindi and Marathi.

Aavinash is a versatile dancer trained in International Salsa and Latin American Ballroom Dances. Aavinash had the privilege to learn the most popular classical dance style- Kathak under Guru Vijayshree Choudhary and he also shows remarkable quality to present the folk culture of India.

Aavinash performed Kathak dance choreography under the guidance of Padmavibhushan Pt.Birju Maharaj at Prestiguious World Cultural Festival,Delhi

Aavinash has worked on many National and International projects.His training in varied dance forms helped him choreograph for many television reality shows, music albums,stage shows corporate and private events
