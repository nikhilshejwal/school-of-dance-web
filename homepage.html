About us

Avinash payal's school of dance is one of the finest schools for performing arts which provide Indian and International, Classical and Folk dance forms under one roof with best of faculties from respective fields.

Main goal of the studio is to provide intensive dance training with an atmosphere that lends itself to learning in a safe, non-judgemental and technically accurate preschooler and adults to learn various dance styles

Our vision is to ensure each dancer with confidence to perform and excel in perfomances whether it is for recreation or to pursue as a professional carrer and present a lively performances that will entertain the audience of all age groups. At Avinash payals school of dance, We believe every person has something to offer you will come as a stranger but leave as FAMILY!
